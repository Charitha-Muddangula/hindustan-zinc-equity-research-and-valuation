# Equity Research & Valuation – Hindustan Zinc Ltd.

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a comprehensive equity research analysis of Hindustan Zinc Ltd., combining qualitative insights with financial modeling.

It integrates business understanding, industry analysis, and financial evaluation to assess the company’s overall investment potential.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To evaluate the company’s strategic position, financial performance, and long-term growth prospects from an investor’s perspective.

---

## 🧩 Key Components

### 1. Qualitative Analysis (PDF)

* Chairman’s Message (strategy & long-term vision)
* Management Discussion & Analysis (industry trends)
* Industry analysis (zinc & lead market dynamics)
* Corporate Social Responsibility & sustainability
* Director’s Report (operations & governance)
* Concall analysis (management tone & risks)
* Recent developments and business updates

---

### 2. Financial Modeling (Excel)

* Company financial data analysis
* Assumption-based modeling
* Valuation and performance evaluation
* Interpretation of financial trends

---

## 📊 Key Insight

Hindustan Zinc demonstrates strong cost leadership, stable demand outlook, and consistent operational performance. However, returns remain sensitive to global metal price cycles and macroeconomic conditions.
